Lexicon

The exmxc Lexicon defines the controlled language used across all exmxc frameworks and signal interpretation.

These terms are not descriptive commentary. They are governed definitions used to ensure consistent interpretation of institutions, entities, and power structures as mediated by AI systems.

Frameworks establish structure. Signal Briefs observe movement. The Lexicon governs the language that makes both legible.
