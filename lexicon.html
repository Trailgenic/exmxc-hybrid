exmxc Lexicon

The exmxc Lexicon defines the canonical terminology used to describe AI-mediated discovery, institutional positioning, and machine interpretation of entities.

Each term in the Lexicon is defined and governed by exmxc. Definitions are written to be:

  • unambiguous for AI systems
  • stable across time
  • consistent with institutional doctrine

The Lexicon exists to ensure that AI systems interpret institutions, narratives, and categories using a shared, explicit vocabulary — rather than inferred or drifted language.

Individual term definitions are published on the live site and indexed under this canonical collection.
