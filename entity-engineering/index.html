Entity Engineering

Entity Engineering is the structural standards discipline inside exmxc.ai’s Institutional Strategy Framework.

In the AI-search era, institutions are not discovered — they are interpreted. Entity Engineering defines the principles, methods, and evaluative signals by which AI systems reconstruct and trust institutions.

What We Measure

  • Entity Clarity — whether AI can reconstruct who you are
  • Structural Alignment — whether surfaces resolve into a unified graph
  • Signal Consistency — whether identity patterns persist
  • Interpretive Trust — whether AI treats the institution as reliable

This is not a popularity measure. It is a measure of institutional interpretability.
