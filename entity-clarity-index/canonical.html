Entity Clarity Index (ECI)

The Entity Clarity Index (ECI) is a cross-industry analytical framework developed by exmxc.ai to evaluate how clearly institutions are interpreted by AI systems.

ECI does not measure financial performance, popularity, or market success. It evaluates structural interpretability — whether AI systems can reliably reconstruct, contextualize, and trust an institution.

What ECI Measures

  • Posture — the institution’s strategic stance toward AI access and reuse
  • Capability — the structural ability of AI systems to discover and interpret the entity
  • Entity Clarity & Capability (ECC) — a composite interpretive signal derived from posture and capability

What ECI Is Not

  • ECI is not a ranking system
  • ECI does not compare performance across industries
  • ECI scores are not published outside official reports
  • The PDF report is the authoritative record

Scope & Structure

Each ECI report evaluates a single vertical to preserve interpretive integrity. Scores appear only within the context of the report itself.

ECI operates as a standards discipline within exmxc.ai’s Institutional Strategy Framework and is governed by the principles of Entity Engineering.
