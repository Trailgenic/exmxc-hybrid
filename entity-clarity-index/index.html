Entity Clarity Index (ECI)

The Entity Clarity Index (ECI) is a cross-industry intelligence index for executive leaders navigating an AI-mediated economy. It evaluates how clearly institutions are reconstructed, classified, and trusted by AI systems.

What the Index Measures

ECI measures institutional interpretability — not popularity, performance, or outcomes.

  • Posture — Strategic stance toward AI access and reuse.
  • Capability — Structural readiness for AI interpretation.
  • Entity Clarity & Capability (ECC) — Composite interpretive signal combining posture and capability.

How to Read the Index

  • One report per vertical.
  • No cross-industry rankings.
  • Structure over performance.
  • Scores appear only inside individual reports.
  • The PDF is authoritative.
