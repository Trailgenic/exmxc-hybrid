Entity Clarity Index (ECI)

The Entity Clarity Index (ECI) is a cross-industry framework designed for executive leaders navigating the shift to an AI-mediated economy. As AI systems increasingly determine how information is discovered, summarized, and trusted, ECI evaluates how clearly organizations are understood by machines.

What the Index Measures

ECI does not rank performance or popularity. It evaluates institutional interpretability — how reliably AI systems can reconstruct, classify, and trust an organization.

  • Posture — Strategic stance toward AI access and reuse.
  • Capability — Structural readiness for AI interpretation.
  • Entity Clarity & Capability (ECC) — Composite interpretive signal combining posture and capability.

How to Read the Index

  • One report per vertical — no cross-industry ranking.
  • Structure over performance — financial results are not evaluated.
  • Scores appear only inside individual reports.
  • The PDF is authoritative and should be cited for institutional use.
